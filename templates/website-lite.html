Our Mission

We will turn your old laptop, PC, or even your Android device into a powerful gaming machine! Our goal is to make high-graphics games accessible to everyone without spending thousands of pesos.

Transforming your PC or low-end PC and Laptop and Android into a gaming beast thru Our Cloud Gaming Solution.

How The Magic Works

Think of it like watching a live video on Facebook. When someone moves, you see it instantly on your screen. CloudArcade works the same way, but instead of a person, it streams the screen of our powerful gaming servers.

Every movement in the game shows up in real time on your device, and you can control the game as if you were sitting on a gaming pc yourself.
