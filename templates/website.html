Our Mission

We will turn your old laptop, PC, or even your Android device into a powerful gaming machine! Our goal is to make high-graphics games accessible to everyone without spending thousands of pesos.

Transforming your PC or low-end PC and Laptop and Android into a gaming beast thru Our Cloud Gaming Solution.

How The Magic Works

Think of it like watching a live video on Facebook. When someone moves, you see it instantly on your screen. CloudArcade works the same way, but instead of a person, it streams the screen of our powerful gaming servers.

Every movement in the game shows up in real time on your device, and you can control the game as if you were sitting on a gaming pc yourself.

What You Need to Get Started

🌐

Stable Internet Connection

A reliable internet connection is essential for smooth cloud gaming experience.

Why it's important:

Since all games run on our powerful servers, your internet connection acts as the bridge. A stable connection ensures lag-free gameplay, crisp graphics, and instant response to your controls. We stream the game to your device in real-time, just like watching a video - but you're in control!

Recommended:

• Minimum 10 Mbps download speed
• Stable WiFi or wired connection
• Low latency for best experience

📱

CloudArcade App

Download our specially optimized CloudArcade app for your device. Installation Guide Video

Available for:

• Android devices (phones & tablets)
• Windows PC & Laptops
• Easy installation process
• Regular updates with new features

What it does:

Our CloudArcade app is optimized to deliver the best streaming quality, minimal latency, and seamless controls. It's designed specifically for cloud gaming performance!

🔗

ZeroTier Network App

A secure third-party networking tool to connect you to our gaming servers. Installation Guide Video

Why ZeroTier:

ZeroTier creates a secure, virtual network connection between your device and our servers. Think of it as a private gaming tunnel that ensures your connection is stable and secure.

Easy Setup:

• Free to download
• Simple one-time setup
• Runs in the background
• We'll provide step-by-step instructions

Future Plans:

As we grow, we're investing in dedicated static IP infrastructure to provide even better, more direct connections to our servers!
